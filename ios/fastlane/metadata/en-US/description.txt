By comparing each fill-up to your lifetime average efficiency, Vroom not only incentivizes you to be more careful about how you drive and use gas but also helps you save money by simply being mindful of how much you’re spending.

With vroom you can:
- Add gas transactions to your personal list
- View average efficiency
- See your odometer reading at a glance
- See how each transaction stacks against your average efficiency
all within seconds and without clutter.
